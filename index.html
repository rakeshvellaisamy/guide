01

Introduction — What is This Business?

அறிமுகம் — இந்த தொழில் என்ன?

An Edible Oil Processing Business (சமையல் எண்ணெய் தயாரிப்பு தொழில்) means you buy oilseeds (like mustard seeds, coconut, sunflower seeds, groundnuts, sesame) and press them to extract oil, then bottle and sell that oil. This is one of India's most stable, essential food businesses — every household needs cooking oil every week!

💡 What is this business?
இந்த தொழில் என்ன?

You buy raw seeds → press them in a machine → get oil → filter it → bottle it → sell it. The leftover solid after pressing is called Oil Cake (எண்ணெய் கேக் / பிண்ணாக்கு) which you also sell as animal feed. Two products, one process!

🎯 Why is this a great business?
ஏன் இது நல்ல தொழில்?
  • Oil is used every day in every home
  • Demand never goes down
  • Government gives subsidy (மானியம்)
  • Simple technology, easy to learn
  • By-product (oil cake) gives extra income
  • Can start small and grow big
📌
Beginner Note (தொடக்கநிலை குறிப்பு): This guide covers ALL types of edible oil businesses — Coconut Oil (தேங்காய் எண்ணெய்), Sunflower Oil (சூரியகாந்தி எண்ணெய்), Mustard Oil (கடுகு எண்ணெய்), Groundnut Oil (கடலை எண்ணெய்), Sesame Oil (நல்லெண்ணெய்), and more. The process is almost the same for all — only the seed changes!

Step-by-Step Manufacturing Process

தயாரிப்பு செய்முறை — படிப்படியாக

This is how oil is made from seeds. Every oil (coconut, sunflower, groundnut, sesame) follows basically the same steps. Let's learn each step like a beginner:

1

Raw Material Procurement (மூலப்பொருள் கொள்முதல்)

விவசாயிகளிடமிருந்து நேரடியாக விதைகள் வாங்குவது

Buy seeds directly from farmers (விவசாயிகள்) or mandis (சந்தை). Buying directly saves 10–15% cost. For coconut oil: buy dried copra (dry coconut). Always check moisture level — wet seeds give less oil and spoil faster.

2

Cleaning (சுத்திகரிப்பு)

கல், மண், தூசி நீக்குதல்

Remove dirt, stones, dust, and foreign particles from the seeds using a cleaning machine (சுத்திகரிப்பு இயந்திரம்) or vibrating sieve (சல்லடை). Dirty seeds can damage the expeller machine and reduce oil quality. Think of this like washing vegetables before cooking!

3

Drying (உலர்த்துதல்)

ஈரப்பதத்தை குறைத்தல்

Seeds must have low moisture for maximum oil extraction. Dry in sunlight (சூரிய வெளிச்சம் — free!) or use a mechanical dryer. Target moisture: below 8%. Wet seeds = less oil + bad quality oil. This step is FREE if you use sunlight!

4

Heating / Cooking (வெப்பமாக்குதல்)

விதைகளை வேகவைத்தல்

Seeds are heated in a cooking/heating kettle (வேகவைக்கும் பாத்திரம்) to around 60–80°C. This breaks the cell walls of seeds, allowing MORE oil to come out easily. It also improves the oil's taste and aroma. Skip this step = 5–10% less oil yield.

5

Oil Extraction / Pressing (எண்ணெய் பிழிதல்)

எண்ணெய் எந்திரத்தில் பிழிதல்

Hot seeds are fed into the Oil Expeller Machine (எண்ணெய் பிழியும் இயந்திரம்). Inside the machine, a large rotating screw (worm) crushes the seeds and squeezes the oil out. The oil flows out through small holes. The solid leftover is Oil Cake (பிண்ணாக்கு). One machine can process 200–500 kg seeds per hour!

6

Filtration (வடிகட்டுதல்)

கலப்படம் நீக்குதல்

Raw extracted oil contains seed particles, moisture, and impurities. Use a Filter Press (வடிகட்டு இயந்திரம்) to get clear, pure oil. This is exactly like using a coffee filter — impurities stay behind, clean oil passes through. This step directly impacts oil quality and shelf life.

7

Quality Check (தரம் சோதனை)

தரம் பரிசோதனை

Check the oil for: colour (நிறம்), smell (வாசனை), free fatty acid level (FFA), moisture content. For FSSAI compliance, you must test your oil regularly. Get samples tested at a NABL-accredited lab (₹500–2000 per test). Good quality = loyal customers + legal compliance.

8

Packaging (பேக்கேஜிங்)

பாட்டிலில் அடைத்தல்

Fill the oil into bottles (500ml, 1 litre, 5 litre) or tins (15 litre) using a filling machine. Seal with caps. Attach your branded label. Pack in cartons for delivery. Packaging is your brand's face — invest in good design!

9

Storage & Dispatch (சேமிப்பு மற்றும் விநியோகம்)

கிடங்கு மற்றும் விற்பனை

Store finished oil in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Oil shelf life: 6–12 months. Dispatch to retailers, hotels, and customers. Maintain stock rotation — sell older stock first (FIFO method).

💡
Wood-Pressed / Cold-Pressed Oil (மரக்கோல் எண்ணெய் / சீதள அழுத்த எண்ணெய்) — Premium Opportunity!
Cold-pressed or wood-pressed (chekku) oil is extracted WITHOUT heating, keeping more nutrients. This sells for 2–3x more price. Example: Cold-pressed sesame oil sells at ₹400–600/litre vs regular ₹200. It's a premium niche with rapidly growing demand from health-conscious buyers!

Machinery & Equipment Guide

இயந்திரங்கள் மற்றும் உபகரண வழிகாட்டி

Here is every machine you need, explained in simple terms. Don't get confused by technical names — we'll make it simple!

🔧

Oil Expeller Machine (எண்ணெய் பிழியும் இயந்திரம்) — ₹2.5–6L

மிக முக்கியமான இயந்திரம்

What it does: The main machine. Has a rotating screw inside that crushes seeds and squeezes oil out. Like a giant garlic press! Capacity: 100–500 kg/hour. Buy from reputed brands: Tinytech, Komet, or local Rajkot manufacturers. Always check the "crushing capacity" — more is better for production.

🍳

Heating Kettle / Cooker (வேகவைக்கும் கலன்) — ₹35–80K

விதைகளை சூடாக்கும் கலன்

What it does: A large round pot with a steam jacket or direct heating that heats seeds before pressing. Heating increases oil yield by 5–10%. Works like a pressure cooker but for seeds. Capacity: 100–300 kg batch.

🔍

Filter Press (வடிகட்டு அழுத்தி) — ₹1–2.5L

எண்ணெயை சுத்தப்படுத்தும் இயந்திரம்

What it does: Raw oil from expeller is cloudy with particles. Filter press uses filter cloths to separate these impurities. Result: crystal clear oil. Think of it like a large tea strainer. Size: 18"×18" with 18 plates minimum for a small unit.

⚡

Electric Motor (மின் மோட்டார்) — ₹60–120K

இயந்திரத்தை இயக்கும் மோட்டார்

What it does: Powers the expeller machine. Minimum 20 HP (horse power) for a standard unit. Buy branded motors (Crompton, Siemens, ABB) with L&T starter for safety. Use 3-phase electricity connection. Higher HP = faster processing but more electricity cost.

📦

Oil Filling Machine (எண்ணெய் நிரப்பும் இயந்திரம்) — ₹80K–2L

பாட்டிலில் எண்ணெய் அடைக்கும் இயந்திரம்

What it does: Automatically fills exact quantities into bottles. Semi-automatic machines fill 500–1000 bottles/hour. Prevents wastage and ensures accurate measurement. Also available: manual tap fillers (cheap but slow) for very small scale.

🧪

Cleaning Machine / Destoner (சுத்திகரிப்பு இயந்திரம்) — ₹50–80K

விதைகளிலுள்ள அழுக்கை நீக்கும் இயந்திரம்

What it does: Vibrating sieve that separates stones, dust, and foreign particles from seeds before pressing. Protects your expensive expeller machine from damage. Optional for very small scale but highly recommended.

⚠️
Important Machine Buying Tips (இயந்திரம் வாங்கும்போது கவனிக்க வேண்டியவை):
1. Always see a demonstration (டெமோ) before buying. 2. Check spare parts availability in your area. 3. Get warranty in writing (குறைந்தது 1 வருடம்). 4. Rajkot (Gujarat) is the hub for oil mill machinery — you can buy directly and save 20–30%. 5. Don't buy second-hand machines for your first unit — too risky!

Marketing & Sales Strategy

சந்தைப்படுத்தல் மற்றும் விற்பனை திட்டம்

Even the best oil won't sell itself. Here's a complete marketing plan from Day 1:

🏷️ Phase 1: Branding (முதல் மாதம்)
பிராண்ட் உருவாக்குதல்
  • Choose a Tamil/Regional brand name (e.g., "Ponni Gold", "Chekku Fresh")
  • Design a logo (₹2,000–5,000 on freelancer)
  • Create bottle labels — mention "Natural", "Cold-Pressed", "No Added Chemicals"
  • Register your brand (Trademark) later as you grow
  • Make 500ml, 1L, 5L bottle sizes available
🛒 Phase 2: Local Distribution (மாதம் 2–3)
உள்ளூர் விநியோகம்
  • Visit 50 local kirana stores — give FREE samples
  • Offer credit terms (30-day payment) to build trust
  • Target ration shops, cooperative stores
  • Approach AAVIN / dairy farmer cooperatives
  • Join local traders' WhatsApp groups
🍽️ Phase 3: Hotel & Catering (மாதம் 3–6)
ஹோட்டல் மற்றும் கேட்டரிங்
  • Hotels buy 15–100L per week — huge volume!
  • Give special bulk pricing (₹5–10/litre discount)
  • Approach catering companies for event supply
  • Provide 15L tins with your branding
  • Build a route (hotel route visits every 2 weeks)
📱 Phase 4: Digital Marketing (மாதம் 4+)
டிஜிட்டல் சந்தைப்படுத்தல்
  • Create Instagram/Facebook page — show your oil making process!
  • List on Amazon, Flipkart, JioMart
  • Create WhatsApp Business catalog
  • Partner with organic/health food influencers
  • List on Meesho for rural distribution

🏆 Your Biggest Marketing Weapon

Show customers YOUR process — people love seeing fresh, natural oil being made. A simple 30-second Reels video of your oil expeller in action can get 10,000+ views and bring real customers. Transparency = Trust = Sales!

Key Financial Terms Simply Explained (எளிய நிதி சொற்கள்):
• Break-Even Point (சம நிலை புள்ளி) = The minimum production at which you cover all costs. At 43% capacity, you just break even in Year 1. So run at 50%+ to make profit.
• DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) = Your ability to repay bank loan. 3.63 means you earn 3.63x more than what you need to repay. Banks require minimum 1.25 — you're at 3.63, which is excellent!
• Working Capital (பணி முதலீடு) = Money needed for daily operations — buying seeds, paying wages BEFORE you sell the oil.

Legal Registration & Compliance

சட்ட பதிவு மற்றும் இணக்கம்

You MUST register your business before selling oil. Here are all the registrations you need, explained simply:

📋

FSSAI License (உணவு பாதுகாப்பு உரிமம்) — MANDATORY

கட்டாயம் தேவை

What: Food Safety and Standards Authority of India license. Without this, you CANNOT sell food products in India. How to get: Apply at fssai.gov.in. For annual turnover under ₹12L: Basic Registration (₹100/year). For ₹12L–20Cr: State License (₹2,000–5,000/year). Takes 7–30 days. Don't skip this — heavy fines if caught!

🏢

Business Registration (வணிக பதிவு)

தொழில் பதிவு

Register as Proprietorship (தனிவணிகம் — easiest), Partnership (கூட்டாளி தொழில்), or Pvt Ltd Company. For starting, Sole Proprietorship is easiest. Just open a current account with your business name. Apply for Udyam Registration (MSME) at udyamregistration.gov.in — FREE and gives you access to government schemes!

📊

GST Registration (சரக்கு சேவை வரி பதிவு)

GST பதிவு

If your annual turnover exceeds ₹40 Lakhs, GST registration is mandatory. Edible oils attract 5% GST. Register at gst.gov.in — free. File monthly/quarterly returns. Benefit: You can claim Input Tax Credit (ITC) on machinery purchase!

💧

BIS / Agmark Certification (தர சான்றிதழ்)

தர சான்றிதழ்

Agmark is a quality certification from the Government for agricultural products including oils. Having Agmark on your bottle dramatically increases customer trust and allows you to sell in government channels. Apply at agmark.dac.gov.in. Costs ₹5,000–20,000/year. Additional Recommendation: Get ISI/BIS mark for premium market access.

🏭

Factory License & Local Permissions (தொழிற்சாலை உரிமம்)

உள்ளூர் அனுமதி

Get NOC (No Objection Certificate) from local Panchayat/Municipality. If hiring 10+ workers, register under Factories Act at your state Labour Department. Obtain electricity connection (industrial tariff) from TANGEDCO/MSEB. Also get Pollution Control Board NOC if required by your state.

🎯

PMFME Scheme Application (அரசு திட்ட விண்ணப்பம்)

மானிய விண்ணப்பம்

Apply for PM Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises scheme to get up to ₹10 Lakh subsidy (35% of project cost). Apply through your District Industries Centre (DIC) or at pmfme.mofpi.gov.in. Also explore: MUDRA Loan (₹10L – no collateral), NABARD schemes for food processing, State government schemes for MSMEs.

6-Month Startup Timeline

6 மாத தொடக்க காலவரிசை

Here is a practical month-by-month action plan to go from idea to earning money in 6 months:

Month 1 — Research & Plan (ஆராய்ச்சி மற்றும் திட்டம்)

Foundation Phase

Visit 3–5 existing oil mills in your area. Talk to farmers about seed prices. Decide which oil type to produce. Identify your location. Open a business bank account. Apply for Udyam/MSME registration (free, online, 5 minutes). Start PMFME subsidy application process.

Month 2 — Legal & Finance (சட்டம் மற்றும் நிதி)

Compliance Phase

Apply for FSSAI license. Submit bank loan application with DPR. Finalize building location and get rent agreement / construction started. Apply for 3-phase industrial electricity connection (takes 30–45 days). Get quotes from 3+ machinery suppliers. Finalize brand name and logo design.

Month 3 — Setup (அமைப்பு நிறுவுதல்)

Infrastructure Phase

Civil work completion: factory floors, oil collection pits, storage area. Order and receive machinery. Install machines with manufacturer's help. Get electrical work done. Purchase initial packaging materials. Print branded labels and bottle caps. Hire machine operator (get experienced person from another mill).

Month 4 — Trial Production (சோதனை உற்பத்தி)

Testing Phase

Buy 500 kg raw material for trial run. Run the expeller machine. Note the oil yield, quality, and any machine issues. Fix all problems. Send oil sample to lab for quality testing. Get FSSAI license number (should arrive by now). Print final labels with FSSAI number.

Month 5 — Pre-Launch Marketing (முக்கியமான மாதம்)

Market Entry Phase

Visit 50+ local kirana stores with free samples. Approach 10–15 hotels and restaurants. Create WhatsApp Business profile and catalog. Set up Instagram/Facebook page. List on Amazon/Flipkart if targeting online. Take pre-orders before full production. Build distributor relationships.

Month 6 — Full Launch (முழு அளவு தொடக்கம்!)

Production & Sales Phase

Start full production at 50% capacity. Deliver first orders to all confirmed customers. Collect feedback actively. Start tracking daily production, sales, and cash flow. Review financial performance against DPR projections. Plan for month 7 expansion of customer base. CELEBRATE — you're a business owner now! 🎉

Common Mistakes to Avoid

தவிர்க்க வேண்டிய பொதுவான தவறுகள்
❌
Mistake 1: Skipping FSSAI Registration — Selling oil without FSSAI license can result in ₹2–5 Lakh fines and business closure. Never skip this!
❌
Mistake 2: Buying Low-Quality Machines (மோசமான இயந்திரம்) — Cheap machines break down often, give less oil yield, and cost more in repairs. Buy from reputed manufacturers with warranty. The machine is your profit-maker!
❌
Mistake 3: Not Testing Oil Quality (தர சோதனை இல்லாமல்) — Selling adulterated or sub-standard oil destroys your brand forever. Test every batch before selling.
❌
Mistake 4: Ignoring Working Capital (பணி முதலீட்டை புறக்கணித்தல்) — Many new businesses fail because they use all money on machines and have nothing left to buy raw materials. Keep 2–3 months of working capital as reserve!
❌
Mistake 5: Selling Only Oil, Ignoring Oil Cake (பிண்ணாக்கை விற்காமல் விடுவது) — Oil cake is 20–25% of your total revenue. Don't throw it away — build buyer relationships with dairy/poultry farms from Day 1.
❌
Mistake 6: No Branding (பிராண்ட் இல்லாமல் விற்பது) — Generic oil in plain cans gets the lowest price. Branded oil can sell for 30–50% more. Invest ₹5,000–10,000 in good branding — it returns 10x.
❌
Mistake 7: Overproducing Before Finding Buyers (வாங்குவோர் இல்லாமல் உற்பத்தி) — Don't produce 1,000 litres before you have buyers for 500. Start small, confirm buyers, then scale up.

Advanced Pro Tips for Growth

வளர்ச்சிக்கான உயர்நிலை ஆலோசனைகள்
🥇 Go Premium: Cold-Pressed / Chekku Oil
குளிர் அழுத்த / சேக்கு எண்ணெய்

Cold-pressed (செக்கு முறை) oil sells at 2–3x regular price. Health-conscious urban consumers pay ₹400–700/litre for cold-pressed sesame or coconut oil. Add a Chekku (traditional wooden press) machine for ₹1.5–3L and open a premium product line!

🤝 Direct Farmer Contracts
நேரடி விவசாயி ஒப்பந்தம்

Sign annual contracts with 5–10 farmers to buy their entire seed crop. Benefits: Lower prices (10–15% savings), guaranteed supply, freshness, and farmers love the certainty too. Win-win partnership!

📦 Private Label for Supermarkets
சூப்பர் மார்கெட் பிரத்யேக பிராண்ட்

Approach local supermarket chains to sell YOUR oil under THEIR brand label. They handle marketing; you get guaranteed bulk orders. Called "Private Label Manufacturing." Start with 100L/week commitments and grow from there.

🌱 Organic Certification
இயற்கை சான்றிதழ்

Get organic certification (NPOP or PGS-India) for your oil if you source from organic farmers. Organic certified oil sells 50–100% higher. Certification takes 6–18 months. Start the process early — plant the seed (pun intended)!

🏪 Open a "Chekku Shop"
சேக்கு கடை திறக்கவும்

A storefront where customers bring their own seeds and you press the oil fresh in front of them. Highly popular in Tamil Nadu. You charge ₹50–100/kg for pressing service. Near-zero raw material cost for this model!

🧴 Diversify Products
தயாரிப்புகளை பல்வகைப்படுத்துதல்

Using the same machines, add:
• Hair oil (hair care products with added herbs)
• Massage oil (ayurvedic blends)
• Pickle oil (special grades)
• Animal feed products (processed oil cake)
More products = more revenue, same fixed cost!
